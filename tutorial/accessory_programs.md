## Accessory Programs

JANAS relies on third-party packages for specific tasks:

- **RELION**: local-resolution estimation, 3D reconstruction. Available at [https://relion.eu/](https://relion.eu/)  
- **IMOD**: basic volume processing. Available at [https://bio3d.colorado.edu/imod/](https://bio3d.colorado.edu/imod/)  
- **pyem**: for importing data from cryoSPARC stacks. Available at [https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.3576630](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.3576630)  

Make sure all are installed and on your `PATH` before running JANAS.

We are currently working to minimise these dependencies by developing integrated and optimised modules to streamline data processing within JANAS.
